Home Page

I am a Lecturer/Assistant Professor at University College Dublin (UCD), Ireland. My research is primarily focused on the mathematical modelling, stability analysis, and control of electric power systems. Interested in working together on a relevant topic? Get in touch!

I was born in Kozani, Greece. I received the Diploma (ME) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA), Greece, in 2017, and the PhD in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from UCD, in 2021. From Jan. to Apr. 2020, I was a visiting researcher at Northeastern Univ., Boston, MA. In 2021/22, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at UCD, working with the EU H2020 project “edgeFLEX”. In 2022/23, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at ETH Zürich, Switzerland, working with the Power Systems Laboratory and the NCCR Automation project.

I have co-authored about 50 scientific publications, including about 30 articles in academic journals, as well as papers in international conference proceedings, technical reports, book chapters, and the book "Eigenvalue Problems in Power Systems" with CRC Press. I am a member of IEEE and CIGRE, a member of the Technical Chamber of Greece, and regularly serve as reviewer and guest editor for academic journals of international standing.
